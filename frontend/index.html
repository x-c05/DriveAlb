Platform Roles

Designed for every user type

Clients

Clients can explore available vehicles, apply filters, view vehicle details, rent cars, or send inquiries to sellers.

Sellers

Sellers can publish listings for selling or renting vehicles, manage promotional offers, and monitor their earnings.

Staff

Staff members validate car listings, maintain brand data, and oversee platform activities to keep listings reliable.

Admin

Admins have full authority over the system, including staff management, user control, and global platform configurations.

System Flow

How DriveAlb works

01

Explore vehicles

Users browse vehicle listings and view the most important information such as brand, model, year, fuel type, city, and price.

02

Apply filters

Clients filter cars by listing type, brand, vehicle category, city, or other important vehicle information.

03

Send inquiry or rent

Interested clients can contact sellers or send a rental request for vehicles that are available for rent.

04

Manage platform

Sellers, staff, and admins manage listings, promotions, approvals, users, staff accounts, and system settings.

Key Features

Main functionalities

Vehicle Listings

Cars can be listed for sale or rent with details such as brand, model, year, mileage, engine, fuel type, city, and price.

Search & Filters

Users can search by keyword and filter vehicles to quickly find the listings that match their needs.

Listing Validation

Staff can review vehicle listings before they appear publicly, helping the platform remain trustworthy.

Role-Based Access

The system separates access for clients, sellers, staff, and admins based on their responsibilities.

# DriveAlb – Online Car Marketplace Platform

Welcome to **DriveAlb**, a comprehensive online platform designed for buying, selling, and renting vehicles. It provides users with a secure and efficient digital space to manage automotive transactions. This project is being developed as part of the Software Engineering course (CEN302) at Epoka University.

## Project Overview

DoMakina is structured as a multi-role system that supports four main types of users:

- **Clients** – Can explore available vehicles, apply filters, rent cars, or send inquiries.  
- **Sellers** – Have the ability to publish listings for selling or renting cars, manage promotional offers, and monitor their earnings.  
- **Staff** – Responsible for validating car listings, maintaining brand data, and overseeing platform activities.  
- **Admin** – Holds complete authority over the system, including staff management and global platform configurations.  

## Key Features

- Car listing management along with status tracking  
- Revenue monitoring and promotion handling  
- Email confirmation and password reset functionality  
- Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) system  
- Administrative dashboards and audit logging  
- Processes for car verification and re-verification
